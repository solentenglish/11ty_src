question: You work for an international company. You have seen an advertisement for an online training course which will be useful for your job. Write an e-mail to the head of Human Resources. In your e-mail — describe the training course you want to do, explain how the company can help you, say how the course will be useful for your job.

answer: |

    Hi John, 

    Thank you for your feedback after my recent performance review. I have been thinking about your suggestion to register for an online course and I've found one that would be perfect. It's a six-month course to get certification in repairing gas boilers.

    The course is completely online so I wouldn't need to travel off-site. I would just need some time on Friday afternoons to complete the assignments as part of my ongoing professional development. At the end of the course, I would be certified to install class C3 gas boilers in both residential and commercial properties.

    In terms of personal development, it would get me closer to becoming a site foreperson. It would also be good for the company as there were a few projects last year that were delayed while we tried to find an outside contractor to do boiler installations.

    Let me know what you think and perhaps we could meet next week to discuss it,

    Thanks
