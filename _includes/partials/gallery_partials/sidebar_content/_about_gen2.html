About General Task Two

In this task, you have to write an essay on a topic of general interest. You may be asked to comment on a situation, express your opinion, or provide a solution to a problem.

The key is to clearly present and support ideas using relevant examples and evidence.

Task 2 assesses the ability to think critically and develop a logical argument.

Success in Task 2 involves understanding the essay prompt, identifying the type of essay required, and organizing the response logically.

You are required to write a minimum of 250 words.
