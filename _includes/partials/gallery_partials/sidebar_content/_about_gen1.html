About General Task One

In this task, you are asked to write a letter. The letter may be formal, semi-formal, or informal, depending on the given scenario.

The scenarios can include writing a letter to a friend, a complaint letter, or an application letter.

The key is to understand the purpose and tone of the letter and respond appropriately.

Success in this task involves including the necessary information, using the appropriate language style, and organizing the content coherently.

You are required to write a minimum of 150 words.
